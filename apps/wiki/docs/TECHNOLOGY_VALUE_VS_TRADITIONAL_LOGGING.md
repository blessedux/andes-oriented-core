# Why AndesOriCore technology matters versus traditional structural logging

## Executive summary

Traditional structural logging often depends on manual interpretation, notebook consistency, and operator-specific criteria. AndesOriCore replaces that fragmented process with a standardized digital workflow where geometry, orientation, depth references, and measurement logic remain consistent from capture to interpretation.

## Traditional method: common pain points

- **Operator dependency:** results vary depending on who logs, how they interpret marks, and how they transcribe values.
- **Limited traceability:** audit trails are weak when edits happen across spreadsheets, photos, and disconnected systems.
- **Rework loops:** inconsistencies between field notes and office interpretation force repeated corrections.
- **Scaling bottlenecks:** quality decreases as teams, shifts, and projects grow.
- **Integration friction:** manually normalized data is harder to connect with downstream geological modeling and decision systems.

## AndesOriCore approach: core advantages

- **Single measurement truth:** canonical rules are reused across Field and Hub for depth, BOH angles, and geometry.
- **Geometry consistency:** cylinder dimensions, FOV alignment, and visible arc mapping are synchronized between capture and 3D editing.
- **Audit-ready workflow:** edits and recalculations can be tracked with explicit logic and repeatable outcomes.
- **Operational speed with control:** teams can process more measurements with less variability and fewer corrective cycles.
- **Better decision confidence:** consistent structural data supports stronger interpretation quality across programs.

## Business and technical value

### 1. Data quality uplift

Standardized calculations reduce interpretation drift and make cross-team datasets more comparable.

### 2. Faster turnaround

Less manual reconciliation between field capture and office analysis means faster reporting and iteration.

### 3. Lower risk

Traceable logic and reproducible calculations reduce error propagation into planning and modeling.

### 4. Scalable operations

The same rules apply across rigs, projects, and teams, improving repeatability as throughput grows.

## Strategic differentiator

AndesOriCore is not only a UI layer over logging. Its key differentiator is a proprietary technical framework that unifies:

- capture geometry,
- depth normalization,
- angular canonicalization,
- recalculation logic,
- and consistent 2D-to-3D mapping.

This creates a defensible operational and analytical advantage compared to traditional methods that rely on manual harmonization.
