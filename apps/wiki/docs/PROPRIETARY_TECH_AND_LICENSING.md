# AndesOriCore: Differentiation, proprietary algorithms, and licensing

## Platform purpose

AndesOriCore is a specialized platform for structural drilling capture, normalization, and analysis. Its core value is converting field observations into consistent, auditable, and comparable geological data across teams.

## Technology differentiators

- Unified Field ↔ Hub workflow so geometry, depth, and orientation remain consistent end to end.
- Measurement standardization around a 30 cm cylindrical reference frame to reduce operational ambiguity.
- Live recalculation of structural parameters when points are edited (`alpha`, `beta`, `dip`, `dip direction`) with full traceability.
- Controlled mapping between 2D composite image and 3D cylinder, including near-hemisphere constraints to prevent incorrect interpretation.

## Proprietary algorithms

The platform includes proprietary logic and methods for:

- trio and capture-depth normalization,
- BOH angle canonicalization and axial orientation,
- geological plane reconstruction from editable points,
- visible-cylinder UV mapping and effective FOV alignment between capture and Hub 3D editor.

These capabilities are part of AndesOriCore know-how and are protected intellectual property.
